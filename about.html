Who We Are

InstanteSevai is a professional registration services company dedicated to simplifying the complex process of business registrations in India. We understand that starting or expanding a business requires numerous legal registrations and licenses, which can be time-consuming and overwhelming. Our mission is to make this process smooth, efficient, and stress-free for entrepreneurs and business owners.

With expertise in various registration services including GST, PAN, TAN, LLP, Private Limited Companies, MSME, FSSAI, Trademark, and Shop Act licenses, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your business needs. Our team of experienced professionals ensures that your registrations are completed accurately and within the shortest possible time frame.
